Who are we?

Cevo are a team of passionate problem solvers, with deep experience in iterative software delivery. We value collaboration, entrepreneurship, curiosity, automation, transparency, community and building quality in to the software development life cycle. Our goal is to build a company of wonderful people who solve interesting problems with great clients.

We began life as a hardware and software reseller. Before being reborn in earnest in 2015 from the devops movement with a group of people who want to see real, meaningful, value-driven change in our organisations.

We believe technology is the single most valuable tool we have for innovation, and we work with our clients to create that climate in their organisations.
